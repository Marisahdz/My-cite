─── ･ ｡ﾟ☆: *.Biography.* :☆ﾟ。 ･ ───

My name is Marisa Hernandez Villasenor, and I am 17 years old. I was born in

the United States but moved to Baja California, Mexico, just months after my birth

due to my grandma's health issues. In Mexico, I attended kindergarten and elementary

until I was about six years old.

When I started school in the United States, I faced significant challenges because of

my inability to speak English fluently. Many of my new elementary school peers took

advantage of my vulnerability and bullied me.

But as soon as I became fluent in English, I seized all the academic opportunities

that came my way, earning a reputation as an "overachiever." Determination and hard

work have been my guiding principles, leading me to my current achievements. I excel

in even the most challenging classes I undertake, maintaining perfect grades. I've also

managed to balance my role as Dance Captain, essentially living in the dance studio,

with my rigorous coursework.

I used the adversity I faced as a powerful source of motivation to become the best

version of myself. I dedicate all my time to my studies not only to satisfy my own

aspirations but also to honor the sacrifices my parents made for us.
